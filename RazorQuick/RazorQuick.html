Why RazorQuick?

RazorQuick provides an avenue for all variations of barbers to search for work, which allows the customer to select from a wide range of hair styles and appointment times.

Services

Accessibility

RazorQuick allows its members to browse a variety of different hairstylists, all at the luxury of your fingertips. When scheduling an appointment customers will be able to browse a variety of styles until they find one that fits their needs.

Increase Job Opportunities

Hair stylist will no longer be confined to the hours and the space of a barber shop or salon. RazorQuick will increase their exposure ultimately increasing their job opportunities.

Availability

Allowing hair stylist to work on their own time (also reducing their costs of having to rent a chair) gives benefit to the customer as they will be able to book an appointment at their convenience.

Freedom
